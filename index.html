Learn

Seventh chords are the foundation of jazz and pop, among other musical genres. Knowing these chords makes it easier to learn additional music skills, such as how to improvise, compose, and sightread music. Acchordion is a simple and fun iOS app that helps you learn these chords. Practicing with Acchordion can help you nail the basics and move on to the fun stuff!

Improvise

Improvisation (soloing) is a key element of playing jazz—and it’s fun! Learning the notes in each chord will help you improvise with confidence, knowing your solos will fit with the tune.

Compose

Using seventh chords in your harmonies and melodies can make your compositions more nuanced, complex, and memorable. Mastering the sevenths will help you understand the relationships between different chords and make composing a breeze.

Sightread

Reading from a lead sheet requires you to know which notes are in each chord. Once you can do this, you will have the capacity to play hundreds of jazz standards and countless pop songs, no matter what instrument you play.
